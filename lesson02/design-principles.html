Alignment

DHL

dhl.com

Alignment is a fundamental principle of design that involves the strategic placement of visual elements so they line up in a cohesive manner. This approach not only creates balance and order but also helps viewers understand the relationships between different elements in a composition, enhancing overall organization and visual appeal. As you can see in the image, the shapes are aligned beatifully, and additionally, the icons and text in the middle, to create a visually appealing design.

Repetition

Deel

deel.com

The repetition principle of design means the repetition of the same or similar elements in a certain design. Repetition can be reflected in the use of the same or similar colors, shapes, lines, fonts, and other elements, and it differs from its other principles because it is not always meant to be noticed.The image portrays how various texts, colours, font have been repeated.
